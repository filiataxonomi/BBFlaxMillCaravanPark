Management Agreement 2021

The agreement between the Shire of Boyup Brook and the Campervan and Motorhome Club of Australia (CMCA) under which CMCA manages the Flax Mill Caravan Park. It was released by the Shire in response to a Freedom of Information request made by Glenda Moroni, dated 20 January 2023.

The document below is the complete FOI release: the Shire's decision letter and FOI application form, followed by the full management agreement. Personal contact details have been redacted; published with permission.
