What is happening at the Flax Mill Caravan Park

Long-term residents of the Flax Mill Caravan Park — workers in shearing, health, road and infrastructure, and people with nowhere else to live during a housing shortage — have been served notices requiring them to leave.

This site gathers the public record in one place so the community can read it: the Council decision, the eviction notices, the law that applies, and the Shire's own monthly reports on the caravan park.

About this record

Everything here is drawn from public documents: Shire of Boyup Brook Council minutes and attachments, Western Australian legislation, and the management agreement for the caravan park. This site adds no private information and names no private individuals.
