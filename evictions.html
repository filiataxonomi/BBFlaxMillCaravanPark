The eviction notices

The notice served on long-term residents, and the Council decision it relies on.

One CMCA officer sits behind both the park's management agreement and the policy used to evict.

On the Shire's own minutes for 28 May 2026, the Guest Stay Policy that triggered the vacation notices was "developed by CMCA," and the consultation listed for the item is, first, the "Manager of Business & Development, CMCA" (then the Shire's Chief Executive Officer and the Environmental Health Officer).

CMCA's Business Development Officer is Sean Constable. His LinkedIn profile describes his role as: "to develop a network of CMCA member only RV Parks across Australia. CMCA is seeking appropriate Council land for long term lease to develop these low cost not-for-profit parks."

The same Sean Constable signed as the witness to CMCA's signature on the park's 2021 management agreement — the agreement under which CMCA now manages the park and issues these notices.
