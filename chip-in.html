About this research

Over the course of this project I have devoted approximately 200 hours to researching legislation, analysing council records, preparing Freedom of Information applications, verifying statutory references, publishing source documents and producing evidence-based public information — and finding the best way to present it all by building this GitHub site. AI research tools assist with locating documents and formatting material, but every legal reference, quotation and factual statement is independently verified against the original legislation or official records before publication.
